About the game


Football Cards - Cup 2014 Edition
This card game is based on the Football Cup 2014 teams and players. The user can choose a country and set up his team by choosing the players and go through 7 rounds to win the Cup in the end. Every player has three properties: attack, defense and speed, each one has different values. The higher value wins the 1 by 1 fights.

Game rules
Before starting your next match, choose 11 players. You can choose only those players who don’t have to miss out matches because of getting cards before or because of sport injury. After you have chosen the players, the luckier one can decide that the thrown coin is going to be “heads” or “tails”.

Playing against each other
During the match 11 players playing against 11 players, fighting one by one. The winner is going to be the one who is stronger in more features. If both players have the same number of strong features, the winner is going to be decided according to the total points. If it is also equal, the winner is the one who started the game. The score can be 11-0, 6-7 either 0-11, it’s for sure that someone is going to be the winner, the match can’t be ended in a tie.

Injuries, Red card, yellow card
During a match, it can happen that players getting injuries randomly, for recovery they have to miss out 1-4 games. In this case, you can’t choose the injured player at the beginning when you field the team. Injured players are missing out for a given time – it is signed on the player’s card. During the match penalty cards are given randomly. Those ones are going to be received after the one by one match. Getting a red card or collecting three yellow cards means the player has to miss out a match.

Joker player
After a certain time a Joker player can appear in the team, who has extraordinary values. By using this card it’s easy to win. These players just appear and after a couple of matches they disappear. During a tournament they can appear several times to help to the team.

Basic functions
The current round is automatically saved by the system so you can go on with playing at the next signing in. The game has 5 different levels: professional/specialist/medium/amateur/rookie. The level can be set off on the main site at “settings”.
